01 · The Problem

No unified view of seasonal performance across EU markets

The Merch EU team had no centralised system to track how seasonal events and cultural themes were driving product sales across five distinct marketplaces. Data lived in raw SQL tables, decisions were reactive, and year-on-year comparisons were manual and error-prone.

[01]

Fragmented data

Order data existed in raw database tables with no thematic categorisation. Identifying event performance required custom one-off SQL queries each time.

[02]

No year-on-year benchmarking

The team could not quickly compare a theme's performance against the prior year, making promotional planning based on intuition rather than data.

[03]

EU ≠ US — market-specific gaps

Existing frameworks were US-centric. EU events like Karneval, UK Coronation, and Bastille Day were untracked. Purpose-built for EU5 with multilingual keyword mapping.

03 · Theme Taxonomy

49 themes across 3 strategic categories

ANNUAL EVENTS

Periodic & Predictable

Events that recur each year. Tracked as growth opportunities with year-on-year comparison. Promotional planning aligned to these peaks.

CONSTANT THEMES

Year-Round Baseline

Themes prevalent throughout the full calendar cycle. Form the baseline of total sales performance and critical for forecasting.

ONE-OFF THEMES

Unique & High-Volume

Events unlikely to recur but generating significant sales volume — UK Coronation or Euro Cup. Captured separately to avoid distorting baseline trends.

05 · Dashboard Design

Two-section QuickSight architecture

§1

Current Week Theme

Displays current week's performance metrics in Merch WBR format. Designed for operational decision-making.

  • 5 embedded tables per view
  • WMQ toggle (Week / Month / Quarter)
  • Marketplace filter (UK, DE, FR, IT, SP)
  • Theme filter with 49 categories
  • 2024 EU Theme % (Units) — % share view
  • Best Seller ASIN per theme
§2

Summary of the Year

Strategic annual view comparing current year against 3 prior years. Used for seasonal planning and budget setting.

  • 3-year unit sales comparison by theme
  • 2023 Theme % by Units (EU) weekly stack chart
  • 2023 Theme % by Units (US) reference benchmark
  • Rolling trend line by theme category
  • Annual event schedule with revenue actuals
  • Year-on-year increment column (LY vs TY)
