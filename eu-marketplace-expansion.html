01 · The Opportunity

Unlocking untapped demand in Tier 2 EU markets

Key Challenges & Solutions
[!]

Challenges Encountered

Poland profitability crisis — Net PPM at 10.57% (vs 23.2% target), driven by Roberto Cavalli negative margins
Germany & France JIT (Just-In-Time) POs at 99%, negatively impacting D2FC delivery performance
DKNY brand profitability drainers in ES & IT — specific ASINs driving Net PPM down 150bps YTD
Confirmation Rate at 77.6% (–1,198 bps YoY) due to supply chain misalignment on new SKU forecasts
Perfect Inbound Defect at 6.14% driven by missing CLI/GTIN onboarding and bagging prep requirements
[✓]

Strategic Solutions Implemented

Poland deal exclusion — Discontinued BFCM & LMD deal participation in PL (projected BFCM Net PPM at –94.6%)
JIT reduction initiative — Implemented rolling forecast sharing & deal planning visibility to reduce JIT POs
SKU-level profitability analysis — Identified and paused underperforming ASINs (DKNY Be Delicious 30ml, DKNY Women 50ml)
Virtual bundle creation — Launched 100ml fragrance + body mist bundles to improve ASP and Net PPM
Inbound compliance training — Implemented CLI/GTIN onboarding process and prep requirement documentation

05 · Business Impact

From zero to €2.49M — Unlocking new revenue streams

The expansion delivered immediate revenue growth while maintaining profitability discipline. By Q3 2025, the EU4 expansion markets contributed 27.4% of total Interparfums PCOGS, validating the strategic investment in Tier 2 European marketplaces.

// COMMERCIAL OUTCOMES

Delivered €2.49M YTD PCOGS across EU8 at +188.54% YoY growth
EU4 expansion markets now represent 27.4% of total brand revenue
Maintained Net PPM at 23.2% despite rapid geographic expansion
Achieved €423K in Deal OPS (+81% YoY) through Prime Big Deal Days & BTS Tier 2 participation
GUESS brand achieved 54% PCOGS share in new expansion markets, validating SKU prioritization

// OPERATIONAL OUTCOMES

Improved D2FC rate to 95.9% in Q3 (+7,130 bps YoY) through inbound compliance training
Confirmation Rate at 78.9% YTD (+2,370 bps YoY) via deal planning forecast & inventory visibility
Reduced SoROOS to 11.92% (–952 bps YTD) improving retail availability across new markets
Onboarded Roberto Cavalli portfolio across all 8 marketplaces with +2,291% YoY deal growth
Implemented virtual bundle strategy (100ml + body mist) to drive ASP and margin improvement

// STRATEGIC LEARNINGS

Poland profitability constraint identified early — strategic decision to exclude from BFCM deals (projected –94.6% Net PPM)
SKU-level profitability analysis revealed DKNY margin drainers (Be Delicious 30ml, Women 50ml) — paused in ES/IT
JIT reduction initiative reduced supply chain friction and improved delivery compliance
Validated phased launch approach — COTY Ireland makeup 2025, fragrance 2026 based on category maturity
Created repeatable expansion playbook for future EU tier 2/3 market launches
