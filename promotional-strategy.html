03 · Pricing Strategy

Navigating Omnibus vs non-Omnibus compliance across EU10

Omnibus pricing regulations require deals to beat the lowest price in the trailing 31 days (including 3P). Non-Omnibus markets (UK, DE, NL) follow different rules. Managing this complexity across 10 marketplaces requires continuous price monitoring and dynamic discount adjustments.

NON-OMNIBUS MARKETS

UK · DE · NL

🇬🇧 United Kingdom 🇩🇪 Germany 🇳🇱 Netherlands
At least 5% off current site price OR £40 / €40 off basis price (Was/List)
At least 15% off basis price (VRP — Vendor Recommended Price)
Must match the lowest price T30D (trailing 30 days)
Deal rerun: Cannot repeat for as long as previous deal duration (minimum 7 days)
OMNIBUS MARKETS

FR · IT · ES · IE · SE · BE · PL

🇫🇷 France 🇮🇹 Italy 🇪🇸 Spain (NEW) 🇮🇪 Ireland 🇸🇪 Sweden 🇧🇪 Belgium 🇵🇱 Poland
5% off Omnibus Price OR £40 / €40 / 200PLN / 400SEK off Omnibus price
Omnibus Price = lowest price T31D (including 3P sellers)
At least 5% off current site price
Deal rerun before 30-day cooldown requires additional discount to beat T31D
